Projects

I began my development journey about a year ago, when I enrolled in a coding Bootcamp. Zero previous experience with web development led to a difficult 6 months at camp, but also a curiosity I now welcome and cultivate daily. I've learned that moments of confusion are in fact parts of the journey, and moreso, markers of progress. I believe the prospects of mobile-development are very encouraging. The affordability of technology has generated a global, egalitarian phenomenon--there are 3 billion smartphone owners worldwide. Information-sharing should sustain its continuous renaissance via mobile applications, creating a better informed, tighter connected, and increasingly empathetic global population. Markets will continue to greatly benefit from mobile development's growth potentials, at both enterprise and personal/social levels. I couldn't imagine a better place to be.
