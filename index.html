I began my development journey about a year ago, when I enrolled in a coding Bootcamp. Having zero experience with programming and web development before I started, the 6 months I spent at camp were difficult, but they awakened a curiosity I now welcome and cultivate daily. I've learned that moments of confusion are in fact parts of the journey, and moreso, markers of progress.

I respect the power of web development; mobile development in particular. The affordability of technology has generated the egalitarian phenomenon of 3 billion smartphone owners worldwide. I believe information-sharing will sustain its continuous renaissance via mobile development, creating a better informed and increasingly empathetic global population. I couldn't imagine a better place to be.
